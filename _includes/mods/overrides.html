Override Mod – High Conflict Risk
  • Conflicts: This mod overwrites game resources. It will conflict with any other mod that edits the same resources.
  • Rule of thumb: If two mods override the same thing, you usually can’t safely use both — pick one.
  • Patch sensitivity: Overrides are often the first to break after a game update and may require a fast update.
  • Symptoms: Missing UI, broken interactions, lastException spam, or “random” glitches can all be conflict signs.
Tip: After patches, test this mod alone first, then add other mods back step-by-step. Clearing localthumbcache.package is recommended.
