General Policy

Development and maintenance of mods take time and energy. If you enjoy my work, feel free to support me on Patreon, Ko-fi or PayPal.

Translations & Collaboration

I am always looking for help to add new translations or to improve existing ones. Providing localized versions makes mods accessible to a wider community. If you want to collaborate, please join my Discord server. I am open to direct collaboration where we can work together to ensure the best quality for your language.

Distribution & Re-uploads

Do not upload my mods to other websites or mirrors. Always link directly to this official page. Re-uploading my work does not "help" the community; instead, you are punishing me as a creator. It strips away my ability to provide support, track issues, and receive the recognition that keeps these projects alive. Stealing traffic from official sites actively hurts the future development of these mods.

Avoid third-party websites. Use official download links only. Downloading from unauthorized sites not only risks viruses and broken mods but also supports intellectual property theft (mod piracy).
