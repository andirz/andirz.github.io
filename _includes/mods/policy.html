General Policy

Creating and maintaining mods takes time and energy. If you enjoy my work, feel free to support it on Patreon, Ko-fi or PayPal.

Translations & Collaboration

I welcome help with translations or improvements of existing ones. If you want to collaborate, please join my Discord server. Direct collaboration allows proper quality control and coordination.

Distribution & Re-uploads

Do not re-upload my mods to other websites, file hosts, or mirrors. Always link directly to this official page. Re-uploads remove my ability to provide support, track issues, and maintain the mod properly.

Downloads from third-party sites may contain outdated files, malware, or modified content. For your own safety and correct functionality, use official download links only.
