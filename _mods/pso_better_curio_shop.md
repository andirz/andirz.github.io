## What does this mod do?
This mod expands the Strangerville Curio Kiosk by adding a wide variety of new and interesting items inspired by different themes and game packs. Your Sims can now discover even more unique curiosities, mysterious goods, and collectible treasures that bring extra depth to the Strangerville experience. It also introduces a stylish and easy-to-use purchase picker menu, making browsing through the expanded selection smoother and more enjoyable.
