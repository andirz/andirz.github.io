## What does this mod do?

The **Selvadoradian Herbalism** mod unlocks the ancient secrets of Omiscan herbal medicine. By combining the **Herbalism** skill with **Selvadoradian Local Culture** knowledge, your Sims can discover and brew powerful magical potions, including the famous poison antidote.

* **Brew Your Own:** Most potions that were previously restricted to the Reward Store can now be crafted manually.
* **Custom Recipes:** Includes a new custom potion, "Hysterical Tiger," with more recipes planned for future updates.
* **Ingredient Focused:** Potions require specific ingredients, which can be gathered through gameplay or ordered via the *Purchase System Overhaul / SimSim Market*.

## Available Recipes

To brew these potions, your Sim must reach specific levels in the Herbalism skill. The list includes:

- **Base Game Potions:** Instant Hygiene, Instant Fun, Moodlet Solver, Potion of Youth, and various emotional potions (Flirty, Confident, etc.).
- **Jungle Adventure:** Selvadoradian Poison Antidote.
- **Custom Content:** Hysterical Tiger.

## Selvadoradian Antidote Update

The antidote has been significantly enhanced for more immersive gameplay:
- **Quality Levels:** Antidotes now have different qualities. **Poor quality antidotes are deadly!**
- **Magical Aura:** The potion now features a visible magical aura.
- **Realistic Taste:** According to local legends, the antidote tastes hideous, and Sims will react accordingly.

## Requirements

* **Outdoor Retreat (GP01)** and **Jungle Adventures (GP06)** are required.
* **Smart Core Script** is required for the main logic.
* **Lot51 Core Library** is required for the crafting and menu systems.
* **Script Mods** must be enabled in your game settings.

## Translations

This mod exclusively uses strings already present in the game's code. It will automatically appear in your game's language (English, German, Russian, etc.) without additional translation files.

## Compatibility

- **No Overrides:** This version of the mod does not overwrite any game resources, ensuring maximum compatibility.
- Works as a standalone addition to your gameplay.
