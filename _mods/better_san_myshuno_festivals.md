## About This Mod
This mod greatly expands the variety of **City Living** festival stalls in San Myshuno. It adds a large selection of new purchasable items inspired by different themes and game packs, allowing your Sims to discover unique and atmospheric objects that capture the specific spirit of each individual festival.
