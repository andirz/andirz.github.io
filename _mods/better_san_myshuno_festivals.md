## What does this mod do?

This mod greatly expands the variety of **City Living** festival stalls in San Myshuno. It adds a large selection of new purchasable items inspired by different themes and game packs, allowing your Sims to discover unique and atmospheric objects that capture the specific spirit of each individual festival.

### Key Features:
* **Expanded Stall Inventories:** Massive variety of new items added to all San Myshuno festival stalls.
* **Thematic Consistency:** Items are carefully chosen to match the unique vibe of each festival (e.g., Spice Festival, Romance Festival, GeekCon).
* **Colorful Picker Menu:** Introduces an easy-to-use, categorized menu to make browsing and finding items faster and more enjoyable.

## Requirements & Installation

For full functionality, you **must** have the following separate downloads installed:

1.  **Smart Core Script** – The technical foundation for script-based features.
2.  **P.S.O. Common Package** – Part of the *Purchasing System Overhaul* series, containing shared assets and configurations.

## Compatibility
* **The Sims 4 City Living (EP03)** is strictly required.
* This mod does not use new strings, so it is compatible with all game languages.
* Fully compatible with other mods in the **P.S.O. Series**.
