## What does this mod do?

High school festival stalls just got an upgrade! This mod expands the **High School Years** festival stalls by adding a wide variety of new items from various game packs. 

Your Sims can now discover an expanded selection of themed objects and festive details at vendor stands, making school events feel more diverse and rewarding.

## Key Features:
* **Enhanced Vendor Stands:** Significant expansion of the items available for purchase during high school festivals.
* **Cross-Pack Integration:** Discover items from multiple different game packs integrated into school festivities.
* **Themed Objects:** Adds more festive details to enjoy during specific school events.
