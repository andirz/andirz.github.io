## About This Mod

**Take It Off: No Shoes! No Accessories!** automatically removes shoes, socks, glasses, and hats from Sims in situations where wearing them looks unrealistic.

The mod was originally based on an idea by **ShuSanR**. After he announced in February 2022 that he would stop modding and playing *The Sims 4*, the mod was fully adopted. The code was completely rewritten from scratch, making this a fundamentally new and independently maintained mod.

## Core Functionality

By default, the mod automatically removes:

- Shoes, socks, glasses, and hats when Sims **fall asleep in beds or sleeping bags**
- Shoes and socks when Sims **swim**
- Shoes and socks when Sims **play in fountains**

Sofas and casual relaxing interactions are **not affected**.

This behavior fixes several cases where the game does not reliably remove footwear or accessories on its own.

## Design Notes

- Designed for **Base Game compatibility**
- Does **not** use Snowy Escape shoe-removal animations
- No EA interactions or resources are overridden

Similar mods exist that rely on overrides.  
This mod intentionally avoids overriding tunings to ensure **better stability and patch safety**.
