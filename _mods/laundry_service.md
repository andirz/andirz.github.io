## About This Mod

Too busy to do the laundry? Does washing and drying take up too much time? The **Soapy Llama Laundry Service** mod has you covered! 

This mod introduces new interactions for laundry baskets, laundry piles, and folded stacks, allowing your Sims to send their dirty laundry to a professional laundromat for a fee. Once the cleaning time has passed, the clothes will be returned directly to your Simâ€™s inventory.
