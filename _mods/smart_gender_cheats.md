## About This Mod
This mod allows you to change all CAS gender settings for all Sims — including active Sims and NPCs — directly in Live Mode. All interactions are available via the cheat menu (hold the Shift key). With just a single mouse click, you can adjust gender-related CAS options in-game without switching to CAS.

## Key Features
You can instantly toggle the following options with a single click:

- **Body Frame** (Feminine, Masculine)
- **Pregnancy** (Can Impregnate, Can Be Impregnated, Both, Neither)
- **Breasts** (Have Breasts, Have No Breasts)
- **Milk Production** (Can Produce Milk, Cannot Produce Milk)
- **Clothing Preference** (Menswear, Womenswear)
- **Toilet Use** (Standing, Sitting)
- **Genitals** (Vagina, Penis) — *WickedWhims users only*
- **WooHoo Preference** (Women & Men, Women, Men, Neither)
- **Romantic Preference** (Women & Men, Women, Men, Neither)
- **Sexuality Exploration** (Yes, No)
- **Romantic Boundaries** (Yes, No)
- **Jealousy Triggers**
- **Facial & Body Hair Shaving**
