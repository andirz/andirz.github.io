## Mod Description

The **Medical Decorations Kit** features a variety of decorative objects with a medical theme, perfect for adding a professional and realistic touch to your hospitals, clinics, or pharmacies. This collection combines updated medical signs with brand-new items to create a comprehensive themed kit.

All objects are purely decorative and can be found in Build Mode. They feature simple tuning, allowing your Sims to use the **"View"** interaction on them.
