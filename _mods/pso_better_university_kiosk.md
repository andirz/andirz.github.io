## About This Mod

The **Better University Kiosk** mod expands the university kiosks at Britechester and Foxbury by adding a wider range of items inspired by different themes and game packs. Your Sims can now discover more useful, decorative, and thematic objects that capture the spirit of campus life and make the university experience feel richer and more immersive.

## Key Features:
* **Expanded Inventory:** A significant selection of new items that enhance the university experience and dorm life.
* **Thematic Variety:** Includes objects from various packs to make the campus feel more authentic.
* **Modern Interface:** Utilizes the improved purchase picker system for a smoother browsing experience.
