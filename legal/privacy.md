# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: February 7, 2026**

The protection of your personal data is important to me. This website is operated in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

---

## 1. Data Controller
Information about the responsible party (name and contact details) can be found in the [Legal Notice (Impressum)](/legal/impressum/). If you contact me via the email address provided there, the data you send will be processed solely to handle your inquiry.

---

## 2. Hosting (GitHub Pages)
This website is hosted via **GitHub Pages** (GitHub, Inc., USA). When you visit this site, GitHub processes technical logs (including your IP address and timestamp) to ensure security and reliability.
- **Data Transfer:** Data is processed on servers in the USA.
- **Legal basis:** Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR.

---

## 3. Domain & DNS (Hostinger)
The domain for this website is managed by **Hostinger**. Hostinger processes technical DNS-related data to resolve domain requests. This is a purely technical process and does not involve tracking or user profiling.
- **Legal basis:** Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR.

---

## 4. Consent Management (Clickio)
We use **Clickio** to manage your cookie preferences. 
- **User Choice:** We respect **Do Not Track (DNT)** browser settings. Optional tracking is only active if you explicitly consent via the banner.
- **Legal basis:** Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR.

---

## 5. Analytics & Ads
- **Google Analytics:** Only active after your explicit consent via Clickio. IP anonymization is enabled.
- **Google Ads:** Currently **not active**. If enabled in the future, it will only run with your prior consent.

---

## 6. External Links
This website links to platforms like **Patreon, CurseForge, and Discord**. Once you leave this site, the privacy policies of those providers apply.

---

## 7. Your Rights
You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data. To exercise these rights, please contact me via the **email address listed in the [Impressum](/impressum/)**.

---

## 8. Changes to this Policy
I reserve the right to update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date.
