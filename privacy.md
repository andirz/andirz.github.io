This website is hosted via **GitHub Pages**, a service provided by GitHub, Inc., USA.

When accessing the website, GitHub may process technical information such as:

- Browser type and version

This data is processed for technical and security purposes to ensure the reliable delivery of the website.

**Legal basis:**  
Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in secure website operation)

## 4. Cookie consent management (Clickio)

This website uses **Clickio**, a consent management platform, to manage user preferences regarding cookies and similar technologies.

Clickio is used to:
- Obtain user consent for optional cookies
- Store consent decisions
- Allow users to withdraw or modify consent at any time

Consent data may include technical information such as consent status, timestamp, and browser information.

**Legal basis:**  
Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR (consent)  
Art. 6 para. 1 lit. c GDPR (legal obligation)

## 5. Cookies

This website uses cookies and similar technologies only in accordance with user consent.

Technically necessary cookies may be processed by the hosting provider to ensure basic website functionality.

Optional cookies (e.g. analytics or advertising cookies) are activated **only after explicit user consent** via the Clickio banner.

Further details can be found in the separate [Cookie Policy](/cookies/).

This website uses **Google Analytics**, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC.

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about website usage, such as:

Google Analytics is activated **only after user consent** via the Clickio consent banner.

You can withdraw your consent at any time using the cookie settings provided by Clickio.

**Legal basis:**  
Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR (consent)
